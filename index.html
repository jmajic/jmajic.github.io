I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Notre Dame. In Fall 2025, I will join the Notre Dame Department of Economics as an Assistant Professor. I graduated from MIT in May 2022, with a PhD in Economics.

My research focuses primarily on macroeconomics and development. Much of my research focuses on misallocation, firm behavior, and firm dynamics in a variety of settings.

You can contact me at jeremy.majerovitz@gmail.com.
