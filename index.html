I am an Associate Economist (postdoc) at the the St. Louis Federal Reserve, and a visiting scholar at Washington University in St. Louis. I graduated from MIT in May 2022, with a PhD in Economics.

I am on the 2023-2024 academic job market. My official MIT website is here.

My research focuses primarily on empirical macroeconomics and development. Much of my research focuses on firm behavior and dynamics in a variety of settings. I also have a significant interest in applied econometrics.
