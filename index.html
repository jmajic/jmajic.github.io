I am an Associate Economist (postdoc) at the the St. Louis Federal Reserve, and a visiting scholar at Washington University in St. Louis. I graduated from MIT in May 2022, with a PhD in Economics.

In July, I will join Notre Dame as a Postodoctoral Fellow in the Building Inclusive Growth (BIG) Lab.

My research focuses primarily on empirical macroeconomics and development. Much of my research focuses on misallocation, firm behavior, and firm dynamics in a variety of settings.

You can contact me at jeremy.majerovitz@gmail.com.

People often ask me how to pronounce my last name. I pronounce it "my-RO-vich," and it can also be pronounced "MY-er-wits."
